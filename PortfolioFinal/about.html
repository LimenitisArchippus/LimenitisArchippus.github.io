Hello, I am Jonathan Pope

Hello, I am Jonathan Pope, an Idaho Native with an interest in programming, game development, and back end development. I find less enjoyment in front end development, but will do it as necessary.
I've been enrolled in computer science classes since my first semester of highschool, and have always preferred backend development as opposed to front end but i don't actually know why.

Interests

Video Games were a big part of my life growing up as it was for many children within my generation. The early 2000s leading into the early 2010s was a golden time for video games.
This has led me to have a great respect for the industry and I hope that I can one day create games of my own. However, game creation is not my career goal. If I were to go the academic route,

Another career idea that is a little more realistic for me, is to learn another language and work as a media translator, whether it be books, music, or TV. I did actively study Japanese in highschool
so it is in my interest to move to Japan. I went there previously for a school trip and loved it there. I do also have a general interest in linguistics and would be interested in seeking a degree in it.

Motorcycles and Two-Wheeled Transportation

A bit of a newer interest, starting this year, has been many forms of Two-Wheeled Vehicles. This includes motorcycles and other forms, such as electric scooters and electric bikes.
I have an electric scooter, named Milo, that I purchased back in May for $1300 that I loved. The fastest I got it was 48 miles per hour and was always enjoyable to ride.
It had many problems however, it was legally questionable to ride on the road(though I was never pulled over), it felt unstable at high speeds, and both of the stock motors have blown out at some point.
When the rear motor blew out, I replaced it, but this whole process took almost two months, through the diagnosis, part location, and replacement. This also cost me $400 in labor and parts.
When the front motor blew out, not even 5 weeks after the rear, it made no since to me to go through the whole process again. And so I purchased my first Motorcycle.
I went out and purchased a brand new Honda Navi, named Ivan, from a local dealership for $2900. Its a small bike, and some would consider it a scooter in a bike shell, but it has been very fun to ride.
